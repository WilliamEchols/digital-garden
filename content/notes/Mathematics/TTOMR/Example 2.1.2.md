Note that $3 | 12$ since there exists $n \in \mathbb{z}$ such that $12 = 3n$; namely $12 = 3 \cdot 4$

Transitive property of the division relation: for all integers, $a,b,c$
$$
\text{if } a |b \text{ and } b | c, \text{ then } a | c
$$
